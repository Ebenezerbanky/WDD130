History

.Early Exploration (1800s) The river that flows through our water rafting site has a rich history dating back to the early 19th century. The first explorers to navigate the river were European settlers, who were drawn to the area's natural beauty and abundant resources. They marveled at the river's crystal-clear waters, rugged rapids, and stunning scenery. Native American Heritage (Pre-1800s) Long before the arrival of European settlers, the river was home to Native American tribes, who revered the waterway for its spiritual significance and bounty. They called the river "Mishoon" (meaning "wild water" in their native tongue), and believed it held powerful healing properties. Our water rafting site is proud to acknowledge and respect this rich cultural heritage. ** Logging and Mining Era ( Late 1800s-Early 1900s)** As the region developed, the river became an important transportation route for loggers and miners. Raftsmen would navigate the treacherous waters, transporting timber and ore to downstream markets. This era saw the construction of rough-hewn cabins and makeshift camps along the riverbanks, which would eventually become the foundation for our modern-day water rafting site. Recreation and Conservation (Mid-1900s-Present) In the mid-20th century, the river's popularity as a recreational destination began to grow. Adventurers and nature enthusiasts discovered the thrill of navigating the river's rapids and exploring its serene stretches. As concern for environmental conservation grew, efforts were made to protect the river's natural beauty and wildlife habitats. Our water rafting site is committed to continuing this legacy of responsible stewardship and sustainability. The Birth of Our Water Rafting Site (1990s) In the 1990s, a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts and experienced river guides came together to create our water rafting site. They shared a vision of sharing the river's beauty and excitement with others, while prioritizing safety, sustainability, and environmental respect. Today, we continue to build on this legacy, offering world-class water rafting experiences to visitors from around the world.....
